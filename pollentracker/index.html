PollenTracker App

About the App

PollenTracker helps you stay informed about local pollen conditions. View current levels for different pollen types (Tree, Grass, Weed) and see a forecast for the upcoming days. Get detailed information on specific plants, track which ones affect you most by pinning them, and filter the plant list based on your needs. Customizable notifications can alert you to changes in pollen severity or provide a daily summary. Widgets keep key information available right on your Home Screen.

Pollen data is provided by the Google Pollen API. Data availability depends on your location. You can check the current Pollen API coverage for details on supported countries and regions.
